Use Case 4: Passenger Exits Elevator

Scenario 4A: Passenger is in the elevator as it arrives at the passenger's desired hallway.

Author name: Sri Harsha Koppaka (skoppaka)

Pre-Conditions:

  • Passenger is inside the car, traveling to desired floor f and hallway b.
  • Car is traveling in direction d
  • All doors are closed.

Scenario:

  1. Car arrives at the passenger's desired hallway.
  2. CarPositionIndicator displays the current floor of elevator.
  3. DoorMotors[b,r] are commanded to open.
  4. CarLight[f,b] for the passenger's hallway goes off.
  5. Doors[b,r] are completely open.
  6. Passenger exits the elevator.

Post-Conditions:

  1. Elevator is at the passenger's floor.
  2. CarLight for the passenger's floor goes off.
  3. Doors[b,r] are open.

Use Case 5: Passenger Triggers Door Reversal

Scenario 5A: Passenger attempts to enter the car as the doors begin to close.

Author name: Sri Harsha Koppaka (skoppaka)

Pre-Conditions:

  • Passenger is outside the car.
  • Car is stopped at passenger's floor f and hallway b.
  • At least one door[b,r] is open.

Scenario:

  1. Door[b,r] starts closing.
  2. Passenger attempts to enter into the elevator triggering DoorReversal[b,r].
  3. DoorMotor[b,r] commanded to Stop and then Open.
  4. Passenger gets into the car as the doors open again completely.

Post-Conditions:

  1. Passenger gets into the elevator.
  2. Doors[b,r] are completely open.
